Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: 2024  |  This policy applies to all users of Libaas e Rang website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information when you use our services and interact with our website — such as when you make a purchase, create an account, or contact us for customer support. This includes your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details required to complete transactions.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to process and deliver your orders, communicate about your purchases, improve our services, and keep you informed about new collections and promotions. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

Your personal information is protected through industry-standard encryption protocols. All payment transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways to ensure your financial data stays safe.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze website traffic. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time without affecting your ability to shop with us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through our Contact page and we'll be happy to help.
