Frequently Asked Questions

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery for local orders.

  • How long does shipping take?

    Shipping times vary by location, but typically it takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders.

  • Can I return an item?

    Yes, you can return items within 14 days of receipt, as long as they are in original condition.
