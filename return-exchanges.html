Return Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return the product within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and in original packaging with all tags attached.

Exchange Policy

Want a different size or color? We're happy to exchange your item within 30 days. Simply contact our customer care team and we'll guide you through the process. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

Non-Returnable Items

Customized or stitched items, sale items, and gift cards are not eligible for return or exchange. If you received a defective or incorrect item, please contact us immediately and we'll make it right.
